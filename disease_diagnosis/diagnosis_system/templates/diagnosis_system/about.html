Disease Diagnosis System

Patients can register and enter their symptoms to get a possible diagnosis. And also get recommended a doctor based on their diagnosis. Doctors can register and enter their contact details and when verified by admin they will be recommended to patients.

Patients can also save their health details which can help increase the dataset to help better predictions in the future.

Naive Bayes classifier is used to predict diagnosis. The Model has a cross val accuracy of around 95%
